### Advanced Feature

Connecting a custom provider with openwork takes four clicks.

- Go to `Settings` \> `Connect Provider` \> `OpenAI` \> `ChatGPT Pro/Plus`

<Frame>
  ![Clean Shot 2026 03 24 At 14 50 22@2x](/images/CleanShot2026-03-24at14.50.22@2x.png)
</Frame>

Then, login into `openai Auth`  with a valid subscription email

<Frame>
  ![Clean Shot 2026 03 24 At 14 50 59@2x](/images/CleanShot2026-03-24at14.50.59@2x.png)
</Frame>

Once you have finished the auth, you can change the model below in the chat.

<Frame>
  ![Clean Shot 2026 03 24 At 14 52 03@2x](/images/CleanShot2026-03-24at14.52.03@2x.png)
</Frame>

You will be able to choose among any model offered by all the providers you're connected to:

<Frame>
  ![Clean Shot 2026 03 24 At 14 52 49@2x](/images/CleanShot2026-03-24at14.52.49@2x.png)
</Frame>
